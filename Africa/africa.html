Trips: Tanzania and Zanzibar

During the summer of 2022 my mom and I took a trip to Africa. The trip was 3 weeks long, and consisted of several safaris followed by 5 days in the island state of Zanzibar. Tanzania and Zanzibar are south of the equator, so this is their winter, although it is referred to just as the Dry Season. It was all booked off of the Signature Safari from Thomson Safaris, with the added Ruaha, Selous, and Zanzibar extensions.

The trip was incredible, I ended up taking about 10,000 pictures during the time we were there. Take a look at some of my favorites!
